# 🚀 Elovia Love - Strategic SEO Blueprint

This document outlines the long-term SEO strategy and architecture for the Elovia Love dating platform.

## 🎯 Indexing Strategy

### 1. Crawl Budget Management
We prioritize high-value public pages and block low-value user-private routes.
- **Priority:** `/`, `/about`, `/blog/*`, `/city/*`.
- **Blocked (Robots.txt):** `/admin/*`, `/dashboard/*`, `/chat/*`, `/settings/*`.

### 2. Sitemaps
The sitemap is dynamically generated by the Express server.
- **Location:** `https://elovialove.onrender.com/sitemap.xml`
- **Logic:** Automatically includes all active blog posts and strategic landing pages.

---

## 🏗️ React SEO Architecture

### 1. Metadata Management
We use `react-helmet-async` to manage head tags.
```jsx
<SeoWrapper 
  title="Dating in Delhi | Find Your Match | Elovia Love"
  description="Connect with verified profiles in Delhi..."
  ogImage="/images/og-delhi.jpg"
/>
```

### 2. Crawler Detection
The Express server uses a lightweight middleware to detect crawlers and serve optimized headers/responses where necessary, avoiding the overhead of Puppeteer.

## 📊 Structured Data (JSON-LD)

We implement rich results for:
- **Organization**: Site logo, name, and social links.
- **Articles**: For all blog posts.
- **Breadcrumbs**: For hierarchical navigation.
- **FAQ**: For trust and safety pages.

Example implementation in `client/src/components/seo/StructuredData.jsx`.

---

## 🔗 Internal Linking Strategy

1. **Breadcrumbs**: Present on every sub-page to establish hierarchy.
2. **Featured Content**: The homepage links to top-performing blog categories.
3. **Contextual Links**: Blog posts link to relevant City Pages (e.g., "Finding love in the city" linking to `/city/mumbai`).
4. **Footer Clusters**: SEO-heavy links in the footer for quick crawler access to all major landing pages.

---

## 📈 Monitoring & Quality Gates

- **Google Search Console**: Weekly review of index coverage and mobile usability.
- **Lighthouse**: Maintain a score of 90+ for SEO and Accessibility.
- **E-A-T Compliance**: All blog posts must have a verified author bio and expert review timestamp.
